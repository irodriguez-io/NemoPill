## Dependencies, Risks, And Blockers

- **Dependencies:** **T-008 merged to `main`** (its `:core` `Result`/`DoseId`/`ClockPort`, `:scheduling` `ReminderAlarmReceiver`/`SchedulingConstants`/`AlarmManagerSchedulerAdapter`, the KSP+Hilt graph, `DemoViewModel`/`DemoScreen`, and the Kover ADR-086 DSL are all prerequisites). If T-008 is not yet merged, **stop and surface it** (see Task Status note). QA `Coverage Sufficient` + Security `Guardrail Pass` + Human-gatekeeper flip to `Approved for apply` before execution (ADR-064).
- **Risks:**
  - **Cold-start event delivery race.** When the alarm fires after process death, the OS cold-starts the app: `NemoPillApplication.onCreate` (registers the listener) and `ReminderAlarmReceiver.onReceive` (publishes) race. *Mitigation:* the `InProcessEventBus` replay buffer (`_context/06 § Ordering rule`) catches subscriber-late delivery — size it small but non-zero and assert the late-subscriber path in `InProcessEventBusTest`. If the demo proves flaky on hardware, record it and route a durable fix (the Room-as-outbox path noted in `_context/04 § Architecture Risks`) to M-004 — do not silently drop the gate.
  - **`goAsync()` lifetime vs. coroutine scope.** The receiver must finish well under the OS ~10-second receiver lifetime per `_context/06 § Timeout rule` (exact bound in `_context/12`). *Mitigation:* bound the publish coroutine to a short timeout; `finish()` the `PendingResult` in a `finally`.
  - **Hilt injection into a manifest `BroadcastReceiver`.** `@AndroidEntryPoint` on a receiver vs. `EntryPointAccessors` lookup. *Mitigation:* Developer confirms the working pattern at execution time (mirror the T-008 ADR-083 placement finding) and records it as an ADR.
  - **`POST_NOTIFICATIONS` runtime grant (Android 13+).** The notification will not show without the grant. *Mitigation:* for the T-009 demo, rely on the manifest permission + a manual grant; the `Result.Err.NotificationsPermissionRevoked` UX affordance is explicitly deferred to M-004. Record the deferral.
  - **`NotificationManagerCompat` under Robolectric.** Confirm the classpath `ShadowNotificationManager` records posted notifications, their channel, and their actions/PendingIntents for the pinned Robolectric version. *Mitigation:* if the shadow does not expose actions as expected, assert via the posted `Notification.actions` array and `shadowOf(pendingIntent)` instead.
  - **Channel-importance posture vs. file 11.** `_context/11 § BR-010 channel-importance` diverges from M3 defaults. *Mitigation:* read the file-11 posture for `reminder_on_time` and match it (on-time is time-critical → `IMPORTANCE_HIGH`); record the chosen importance as an ADR.
- **Blockers: none** beyond the T-008-merge dependency above.
