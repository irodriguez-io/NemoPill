- Recovery from partial implementation: the most likely partial-implementation failure mode is conversation-context exhaustion in the future T-002 session before all 11 file-11 sections are drafted (file 11's content surface is larger than file 12's because of the four-channel BR-010 copy authoring, the bilingual hard-delete dialog copy, and the English Privacy Policy substance; the section-by-section drafting protocol's context-cost concentration in the early sections compounds with content-authoring's per-paragraph cost).
