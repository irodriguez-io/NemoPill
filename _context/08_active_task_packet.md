- Recovery from partial implementation: the most likely partial-implementation failure mode is conversation-context exhaustion in the future T-001 session before all 10 file-12 sections are drafted (the section-by-section drafting protocol concentrates context cost in the early sections — establishing the project's environment vocabulary, restating constraints — but section sprawl can still push the conversation past usable context length).
